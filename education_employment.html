Currently I am studying these modules in Information Technology:

  • Interactive Digital Media (COMP506)

    This module aims to teach students to work in a team to apply concepts of business analysis, user experience, and interface design to create accessible interactive digital media.

    Learning outcomes include:

    • Create accessible interactive digital media.
    • Apply knowledge and concepts of business analysis.
    • Apply knowledge and concepts of user experience and interface design.

  • Fundamentals of Programming and Problem Solving (COMP502)

    This course introduces students to the principles of problem-solving, and software development techniques to create simple working applications. The Python programming language will be used in the course.

    Learning outcomes include:

    • Apply programming concepts and tools.
    • Differentiate between procedural and object oriented programming.
    • Apply the Principles of Implementation to the development of simple applications.
    • Use problem-solving and decision making techniques which include logic and mathematical concepts, problem solving methods, critical thinking, abstract reasoning, and systems.

  • Fundamentals of Information Systems (INFO507)

    This module aims to apply the fundamentals of information system concepts and practice, including business concepts, development life cycles, data modelling and administration, to support and enhance organisational processes and systems.

    Learning outcomes include:

    • Demonstrate an understanding of the impact of Information Technology on business functions
    • Apply critical analysis and decision-making techniques to solve an organisational IT problem and provide a timely and relevant solution.
    • Apply information and management techniques for the secure storage and retrieval of data and reports from a simple multiple table database to support an organisational process.
    • Apply elementary Agile and DevOps principles and teamwork to design and develop a solution.

  • IT Operations (INFO501)

    This course aims to give a broad understanding of the installation, maintenance and repair of computing hardware and operating systems. It covers the theory and practical material for CompTIA A+ industry certification.

    Learning outcomes include:

    • Explain computer operating systems, configuration, security and software.
    • Configure Windows Operating system on a PC.
    • Explain computer hardware, virtualisation, cloud computing and troubleshooting.
    • Disassemble and reassemble a PC to working order with an operating system and network services.
